# Submissions

## How to submit your work

1.  Open your game in PropellerIDE.

    ![](images/submit1.png)

    <br/>

1.  Click Project Archive button.

    ![](images/submit2.png)

    <br/>

1.  Save your project somewhere you can find it. It will be saved as a `.zip` file.

    ![](images/submit3.png)

    <br/>

1.  Send the archive to your teacher.
